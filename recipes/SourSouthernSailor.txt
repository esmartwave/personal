Title: Sour Southern Sailor
Description: Made this a Roots. The Bartender (Robbie) was super helpful.
Source: Matt
Original URL: 
Yield: 1 serving
Active: 
Total: 
Ingredients:
	1 Tbsp Sugar (or Agave, Raw)
	1 Part Rum (Sailor Jerry's)
	4 Parts Grapefruit Juice (freshly pressed)
	1 Bunch of Mint
	Splash of Soda

Instructions:
	Muddle mint, sugar and rum together. toss in the grapefruit juice. splash it with soda.
