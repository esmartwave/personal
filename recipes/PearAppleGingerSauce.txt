Title: Pear Apple Ginger Sauce
Description: 
Source: food.com
Original URL: http://www.food.com/recipe/crock-pot-apple-pear-sauce-with-ginger-452829
Yield: 4 cups
Active: 30 minutes
Total: 5hrs 30 minutes
Categories: simple, sweet, pear, apple
Ingredients:
	6 large ripe pears, peeled, cored and cut into large chunks
	6 large granny smith apples, peeled, cored and cut into large chunks
	1/4 cup honey
	1/2 cup water
	2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
	1 chunk fresh ginger, about 3 inches long, peeled and minced
	3 tablespoons butter

Instructions:
	Combine the apples, pears, honey (you can leave this out and still get delicious sauce), water, lemon juice, and ginger in the slow cooker. Cover and cook until the fruit is extremely tender and falling apart, on low for 5 hours
	Beat to make a corse mash or use a food processor for a smoother sauce (donâ€™t over blend). Stir in the butter. Serve immediately or refrigerate and serve cold.
