Ingredients:
	1 T sugar
	1 tsp salt
	2 tsp paprika
	1/4 tsp fresh cracked pepper
	1 tsp garlic powder (or one fresh clove pressed)
	1/4 cup vinegar or lemon juice
	3/4 cup salad oil
	1 T minced onion

Instructions:
	Combine dry ingredients in sealable jar. Add liquids and shake the shit out of it for 2 mins.
