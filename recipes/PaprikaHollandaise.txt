Title: Paprika Hollandaise
Description: 
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 2 servings
Active: 15
Total: 15
Categories: egg, sauce, breakfast
Ingredients:
	2 egg yolks
	1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
	1 tsp white vinegar
	1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted (1/2 stick)
	1 tsp hot paprika
	Pinch salt
	Pinch white sugar

Instructions:
	In a steel bowl, whisk egg yolk vigorously until smooth.
	Add vinegar/lemon mix and whisk until thickened.
	Melt butter, add sugar.
	Place egg mixture in bowl over double boiler (improvise or get one).
	Whisk constantly and briskly until a smooth consistency with almost no bubbling. Don't let it firm up!
	Pull bowl away from heat, add paprika and salt. Whisk in.
	Keep stirring the mixture until you serve it.
