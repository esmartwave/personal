Antibacterial Clothing Spray (Witch Hazel + Essential Oils)

Ingredients
- 2 cups witch hazel (alcohol-based preferred)
- 12 drops tea tree oil
- 12 drops eucalyptus oil
- 12 drops lavender oil
- 2 tablespoons vodka (optional, for faster drying)

Instructions
1. Add the witch hazel to a clean 16–20 oz spray bottle.
2. Add the tea tree oil, eucalyptus oil, and lavender oil.
3. Add the vodka if using.
4. Close the bottle tightly.
5. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds to disperse the oils.
6. Shake before each use.
7. Spray lightly onto clothing from 8–12 inches away.
8. Allow the fabric to dry completely before wearing or storing.

Notes
- This is an antibacterial and antifungal reducing spray, not an EPA-grade disinfectant.
- Avoid use on silk, rayon, or other delicate fibers unless spot-tested first.
- Do not saturate fabric. Use a light mist only.
- Store away from heat and light.
