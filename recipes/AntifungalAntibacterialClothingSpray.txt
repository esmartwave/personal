Antibacterial Clothing Spray (High-Witch-Hazel Variant, Metric)

Ingredients
- 470 mL witch hazel (alcohol-based preferred)
- 157 mL distilled or boiled and cooled water
- 16 drops tea tree oil
- 16 drops eucalyptus oil
- 16 drops lavender oil
- 20 mL 70% isopropyl alcohol (see Notes for 91%+)

Instructions
1. Add the witch hazel and water to a clean 700–750 mL spray bottle.
2. Add the tea tree, eucalyptus, and lavender oils.
3. Add the isopropyl alcohol.
4. Close the bottle tightly.
5. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds to disperse oils.
6. Shake before each use.
7. Spray lightly onto clothing from 20–30 cm away.
8. Allow fabric to dry fully before wearing or storing.

Notes
- If using 91–99% IPA, dilute 5 mL IPA with 10 mL water, then use 20 mL of that mixture.
- Essential-oil concentration is matched to the prior formula (same potency per mL).
- Safe for cotton, synthetics, and denim; spot test delicate fabrics.
- Not an EPA-grade disinfectant.
