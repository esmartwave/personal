Title: Danny's Bourbon Pecan Pie
Description: Dad's old recipe. Can make it in an iron skillet as a crumble without crust.
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 1 pie
Active: 
Total: 
Ingredients:
	1 tbsp powder sugar
	4 large eggs
	1 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
	1/2 cup melted butter
	1/2 cup granulated sugar
	1/2 cup chopped pecans
	1 1/2 cup pecan halves (some for topping)
	2 tbsp flour
	2 tbsp milk
	1 1/2 tbsp bourbon (or vanilla extract)

Instructions:
	Preheat oven to 325.
	Bake 30 minutes at 325.
	Bake another 3 minutes at 300.
	Turn oven off, let stand in oven.
