Title: Corn Salad with Creamy Italian Dressing
Description: 
Source: Allrecipes.com
Original URL: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/corn-salad-with-creamy-italian-dressing/?scale=4&ismetric=0
Yield: 6 servings
Active: 20 mins
Total: 35 mins
Categories: italian, corn, red pepper
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/d140a165035141958b765fedba1526ff.jpg
Ingredients:
	Dressing:
	1/3 cup mayonnaise
	2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
	2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons olive oil
	5/8 clove garlic, crushed
	1 to 1/4 teaspoons water
	1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
	1/4 teaspoon white sugar
	1/8 teaspoon salt
	1/8 teaspoon Italian seasoning
	5/8 pinch cayenne pepper, or more to taste
	olive oil
	5/8 (16 ounce) package frozen sweet corn, thawed
	2/3 cup diced roasted red peppers
	salt and ground black pepper to taste
	3 to 1/4 fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced, or more to taste
	5/8 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Instructions:
	Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, 1/4 cup olive oil, garlic, water, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, and a pinch cayenne pepper together in a bowl until dressing is thick and creamy. Cover dressing with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 8 hours or overnight.
	Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir corn in hot oil until light golden brown and toasted, about 15 minutes.
	Mix corn and red pepper together in a bowl. Pour enough dressing over corn mixture to coat completely. Season with salt and black pepper.
	Stir basil and a pinch cayenne pepper into corn mixture; toss.
