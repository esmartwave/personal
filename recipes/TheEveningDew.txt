Ingredients:
	2 oz gin
	1 oz lemon chamomile infused vodka
	0.5 oz st Germaine
	1 tsp smashed sage
	1/4 oz meyer lemon juice (keep the rind for the shaker)

Instructions:
	Smash the sage
	Toss everything into the shaker with some ice
	Shake it and serve it up, hold off on the fresh sage on top unless people really want that
