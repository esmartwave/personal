Title: Best Ever Pie Crust
Description: 
Source: Allrecipes.com
Original URL: http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/best-ever-pie-crust/detail.aspx
Yield: 2 pie crusts
Active: 20 Min
Total: 20 Min
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/355453.jpg
Ingredients:
[optional]
	can substitute shortening for 1/2 butter 1/2 coconut oil
	can substitute flour for GF flour

	2 cups all-purpose flour
	1 teaspoon salt
	1 cup shortening
	1/2 cup water

Instructions:
	In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in water until mixture forms a ball. Divide dough in half, and shape into balls. Wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
	Roll out dough on a floured counter. Don't over work it. Use as directed in pie recipe.
