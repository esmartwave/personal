Title: Red Cabbage Salad II
Description: 
Source: Allrecipes.com
Original URL: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/red-cabbage-salad-ii/
Yield: 6 servings
Active: 15 mins
Total: 15 mins
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/685869a43a214d60970252ff5e066d28.jpg
Ingredients:
	1/2 cup canola oil
	2/3 cup red wine vinegar
	1 tablespoon white sugar
	1 teaspoon salt
	1 teaspoon seasoned salt
	1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
	1/4 teaspoon onion powder
	1 head red cabbage, cored and shredded

Instructions:
	In a bowl, mix the canola oil, red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, seasoned salt, pepper, and onion powder. Place the cabbage in a large glass bowl. Pour dressing over cabbage, and toss to coat. Cover, and refrigerate 8 hours, or overnight, stirring occasionally. Drain before serving.
