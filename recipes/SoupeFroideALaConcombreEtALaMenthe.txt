Title: Soupe Froide A La Concombre Et A La Menthe
Description: 
Source: Laurel Osman
Original URL: 
Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: soup, cold
Ingredients:
	2 large cucumbers, peeled and seeded
	Salt and Pepper
	150g crème fraiche
	1/2 bunch of mint, keep tips for garnis

Instructions:
	Blend cucumbers with the mint until smooth. Add the salt, Pepper and crème fraiche and blend just until everything is well mixed. Do not overblend or the mixture will seperate.
