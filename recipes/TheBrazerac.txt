Title: The Brazerac
Description: A drink I came up with for the France 2013 trip.
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 1 drink
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: alcohol, brandy, absinthe
Ingredients:
	1/2 oz Absinthe Verte
	1/2 oz Luxardo cherry syrup
	1.5 oz Brandy or Cognac
	1 oz water
	A squinch of lemon juice
	A thin slice of lemon peel

Instructions:
	Put absinthe, water, brandy, lemon juice and cherry syrup in shaker with ice.
	Shake firmly.
	Pour in rocks or martini glass
	Place lemon slice in glass
	enjoy
