Title: Honey Crème Fraîche Sauce
Description: Sweet cream topping sauce made with honey and crème fraîche
Source: 
Original URL: http://laylita.com/recipes/2012/11/19/honey-creme-fraiche-sauce/
Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: dessert, honey
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/f81f3840bb3d49398cb3e7e64e5814bd.jpg
Ingredients:
	8 oz. crème fraîche
	1 to 2 tbsp. honey, more or less based on your preference

Instructions:
	Mix the crème fraîche and the honey together using a spoon until well mixed.
	Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to use
