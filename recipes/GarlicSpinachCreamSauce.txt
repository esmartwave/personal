Title: Garlic Spinach Cream Sauce
Description: Modified from the food.com recipe
Source: Food.com
Original URL: http://www.food.com/recipe/fettuccine-with-spinach-cream-sauce-62538
Yield: 4 servings
Active: 
Total: 25 mins
Categories: italian, sauce, spinach
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/1476506.jpg
Ingredients:
	3 tablespoons butter
	2 to 3 cloves of garlic
	1 cup fresh mushrooms , sliced 1/4 inch
	1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
	1/4 cup grated asiago cheese
	1 1/2 cups half-and-half
	1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach , thawed, drained
	3 Roma Tomatoes
	1 teaspoon basil leaves
	1 teaspoon oregano
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1/4 teaspoon pepper

Instructions:
	in 10" skillet melt butter; add mushrooms.
	Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are tender (2 to 3 minutes).
	Stir in flour until smooth and bubbly (1 minute).
	Stir in remaining ingredients
	Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until heated through (6 to 8 minutes).
