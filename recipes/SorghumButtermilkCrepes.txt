Title: Sorghum Buttermilk Crepes
Description: 
Source: 
Original URL: http://seaweedsnacks.blogspot.com/2010/11/sorghum-buttermilk-crepes.html
Yield: 10 crepes
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: sorghum, gluten-free, breakfast
Ingredients:
[Buttermilk]
	Milk (just under one cup)
	1 Tablespoon white vinegar or lemon juice

	1/2 cup sorghum flour
	1/3 cup sorghum flour
	1/2 tsp. salt
	2 eggs
	2 tbls. extra virgin olive oil
	1 cup buttermilk

Instructions:
[Buttermilk]
	Add together, let sit for 5 minutes

[Crepes]
	Add all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth
	Let sit for at least an hour or overnight in fridge before using
