Title: Peanut Butter Satin
Description: Via Victoria Meyer aka Tory via Alissa
Makes good "fat bomb" frozen
Source: 
Original URL:

Active: 
Total: 
Ingredients:
	1/2 C. peanut butter
	1/2 C. butter, softened
	1/4 C. honey
	1 t. vanilla
	3 or 4 eggs

Instructions:
	Cream peanut butter, butter, honey and vanilla in mixer bowl. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating at high speed for at least 5 minutes after each addition. If mixture does not whip up as desired, be sure you have beaten for the full 5 minutes or chill well and then finish beating. May be used as a pie or torte layer filling. May substitute brown sugar for honey.
