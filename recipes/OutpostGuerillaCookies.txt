Title: Outpost Guerilla Cookies
Description: Originally a recipe from Ted Odell in the 1960s. Found in the Graze magazine.
Source: Graze
Original URL: 
Yield: 48 Cookies
Active: 
Total: 
Ingredients:
[Finishing Ingredients]
	1/3 C dry milk powder or whey powder
	1/3 c wheat germ
	2 T millet seeds
	1/2 t cinnamon
	1/2 t salt
	1/4 t baking soda
	1 t brewer's yeast (or 1 1/2 t nutritional yeast)
	1/2 c whole wheat flour (or sub whole and white flour for mixed GF)
	1/4 C white flour
	1/2 C carob or chocolat chip (optional)

[Wet Ingredients]
	1 C honey
	3 T molasses or sorghum syrup
	1/2 C oil or melted butter
	1 T vanilla extract
	2 T nut butter (peanut, almond, etc)
	2 large eggs

[Base Ingredients]
	3 C regular rolled oats
	1/4 C raw sunflower seeds
	1/4 C raw walnuts
	1/4 C raisins

Instructions:
[Dough]
	Make the base - place oats, sunflower seeds, walnuts and raisins in food processor and pulse until well chopped.
	Combine wet ingredients in another bowl, whisk until smooth.
	Add the base to the wet and stir until well combined.
	In separate bowl, combine all the finishing ingredients with the exception of the white flour
	Add the Finishing ingredients to the Base and Wet mixture.
	Then add the white flour a little at a time until the dough is soft but not as sticky (should still have some form but be moist)
	Fold in carob or chocolate chips if using.
	Chill the dough for an hour or more to make it easier to work with.

[Cooking]
	Preheat oven to 350
	Scoop out the dough into golf ball sized mounds on a cookie sheet
	flatten down the cookies a bit
	Bake 15 minutes or until lightly browned with a thin, crispy darker edge.
	Take out, cool and store in a covered container or stack into baggies
