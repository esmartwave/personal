Title: Chicken Joojeh
Description: Persian recipe. Marinated chicken grilled then eaten however.
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 2
Active: 10
Total: 
Ingredients:
	1 lb boneless, skinless, boneless chicken breasts
	1/2 cup olive oil, plus 1 tablespoon warm water
	1 large yellow onion, grated
	1/tsp saffron (about 10 strands)
	1 teaspoon salt
	1 teaspoon pepper
	1/8 paprika

Instructions:
	Chop the onion, throw everything into a ziploc and let it sit for 1-2 days. Cubing the chicken is good for kebabs, or do it whole for sammies.
