Ingredients:
	1 cup whole wheat flour
	2 tsp baking powder
	1/3 tsp baking soda
	1/2 tsp salt
	2 1/2 tablespoons brown sugar
	1/4 tsp. nutmeg
	1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
	1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
	1 egg
	2 Tbsp. butter, melted
	1/2 tsp. vanilla
	1 cup milk (or buttermilk)
	1/4 cup apples, grated
	1 cup diced peeled apple

Instructions:
	For pancakes, combine flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, and nutmeg in mixing bowl.
	In a separate bowl, mix baking soda, egg, butter, vanilla, and milk.
	Combine both mixtures stirring only until blended. Fold in grated apple
	Bake on hot, lightly greased griddle.
	When batter is full of holes, turn to brown on other side.
	Turn pancakes only once while cooking.
