Title: Oven Baked Goat Cheese and Salsa Snack
Description: Lovely, heavy dish!
Source: Kelley Kalivoda
Original URL: 
Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: cheese, goat, mexican
Ingredients:
	8 oz Goat Cheese
	Cream Cheese (to consistency)
	handful of pine nuts
	1 can medium heat salsa

Instructions:
	mix up the cheese to consistency. mix in the pine nuts.
	put it on a plate.
	cover with salsa.
	Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
	Sprinkle with cilantro.
