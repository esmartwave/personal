Title: Tuiles A La Pistache
Description: 
Source: Laurel Osman
Original URL: 
Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: dessert, pistachio
Ingredients:
	3 egg whites
	45g melted butter
	75g sugar
	15g flour
	50g pistachios, crushed

Instructions:
	Combine sugar and flour in a bowl, add egg whites and butter and whisk until well blended. Pour out onto a cookie sheet covered in wax paper or a silpat. Sprinkle crushed pistachios across the surface and cookie in a preheated oven at 150C until golden (7-8 min).
