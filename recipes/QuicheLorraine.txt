Title: Quiche Lorraine 
Description: Cream & Bacon Quiche
Source: Julia Child
Original URL: gratinee.com
Yield: 4-6 Servings
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: breakfast, brunch, egg, bacon
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/355338.jpg
Ingredients:
	3 to 4 ounces lean bacon
	8 -inch partially cooked pastry shell
	4 eggs
	1 1/2 cups cream
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	pinch of pepper
	pinch of nutmeg
	1 to 2 tablespoons butter cut into pea-sized dots

Instructions:
	1) Preheat oven to 375F. Brown bacon in a skillet. Drain on paper towels and press pieces into bottom of pastry shell.
	2) Beat the eggs, cream, and seasonings in a mixing bowl until blended. Check seasonings. Pour into pastry shell and distribute butter pieces on top.
	3) Set in upper third of preheated oven and bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until the quiche has puffed and browned. Slide quiche on a hot platter and serve.

Notes: To pre-bake crust, put in preheated oven at 475 degrees for 10-15 min until crust is set but soft. Make sure to weight it down.
