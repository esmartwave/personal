Categories: hot, indian, spice
Ingredients:
	1 Tbsp black peppercorns, crushed
	1 tsp white pepper
	2 tbsp toasted cumin seed, crushed
	4 tsp crushed coriander
	1 tbsp toasted fennel seeds, crushed
	3 Tbsp ground turmeric root
	4 to 5 green curry leaves (or 1 tbsp green like hyssop, ground)
	1 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
	2 tsp ground cayenne
	1 tsp ginger root, ground
	1 tsp sharp paprika

Instructions:
	Warm in a dry pan and add garlic or ginger for extra fun.
