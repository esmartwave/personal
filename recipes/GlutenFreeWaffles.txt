Title: Gluten Free Waffles
Description: 
Source: 
Original URL: http://themommybowl.com/2010/07/21/bumpas-waffles-the-gluten-free-dairy-free-version/
Yield: 4-6
Active: 15min
Total: 35 min
Categories: sorghum, breakfast
Ingredients:
	2 x-large eggs, whites and yolks separated
	1 1/2 cups non-dairy milk (I used almond)
	1/3 cup melted coconut oil
	2 T. maple syrup
	1 1/4 cups sorghum flour
	1/2 cup arrowroot starch
	1/2 t. xanthan gum (optional)
	2 t. baking powder
	1/2 t. baking soda
	1/4 t. salt

Instructions:
	Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form. Set aside.
	In a large bowl, mix the egg yolks, milk, coconut oil and maple syrup.
	In a small bowl, combine the dry ingredients.
	Mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until no lumps remain. Fold in the whipped egg whites.
	Cook according to your waffle iron specifications.
