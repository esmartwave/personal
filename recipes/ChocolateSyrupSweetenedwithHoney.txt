Title: Chocolate Syrup Sweetened with Honey
Description: This recipe is a great alternative to store-bought chocolate syrup that is usually laden with sugar, high fructose corn syrup, and other unhealthy ingredients.
Source: 
Original URL:

Active: 
Total: 
Ingredients:
	1 1/8 C. honey
	1 C. natural cocoa powder
	1/8 t. salt
	2 t. vanilla
	1/2 C. water

Instructions:
	Stir ingredients together in a 2 quart saucepan over medium heat until thoroughly dissolved and bubbling. Cool and then pour into an empty condiment bottle and store in the fridge. Use 2-3 T. per cup of milk or pour over desserts and icecream for a delicious, healthy treat.
