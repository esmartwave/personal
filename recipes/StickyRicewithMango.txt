Title: Sticky Rice with Mango
Description: From Andrea's visit to NuGensis Farm
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 8 servings
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: dessert, mango, peru
Ingredients:
[optional]
	toasted walnuts

	1 c sushi rice
	1 15 oz can coconut milk
	1 1/2 c water
	1/4 tsp salt
	1/4 c brown sugar
	2 mangoes (or 1 c mango pulp)

Instructions:
	Soak rice for 1 hour and drain
	Combine all ingredients in a pot or rice cooker
	Stir and bring to a boil over moderate heat
	Simmer covered for about 15 minutes until most of the liquid is absorbed
	Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes
	Service with slices of fresh mango or us mango pulp as a sauce
	Garnish with a mint leaf or toasted nuts or coconut
