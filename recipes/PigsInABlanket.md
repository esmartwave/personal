# Pigs in a Blanket

## Yield
Many piggies

## Description
These are weenies in a blanket. what more is there to say?

## Ingredients

### For either version
- Cocktail sausages (or chipolatas): 16–20
- Black pepper (optional)

### US version
- Puff pastry
- Some sort of bar-b-que sauce

### UK version
- Thin-cut streaky bacon: 8–10 slices, halved

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C).
2. Cut bacon slices in half lengthwise, or make small triangular strips of puff pastry.
3. Wrap each sausage with bacon or puff pastry; seam-side down.
4. Place on parchment-lined tray.
6. Roast 20–25 minutes, turning once, until bacon or pastry is crisp.
7. Holding note: Can be kept warm at low oven temperature (170–180°F) for up to 30 minutes without quality loss.

### Simple version
1. Drop the weenies into a pot (or even better, a crock pot)
2. Put your favorite bar-b-que sauce in that pot, just enough to almost cover the weenies
3. slow simmer those weenies 
4. enjoy the simplicity.

## Notes
- Improves with aging.
- Serve with cream, custard, or brandy sauce.
