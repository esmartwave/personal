Title: Soupe Froide Aux Betteraves, Fromage De Brebis
Description: 
Source: Laurel Osman
Original URL:

Active: 
Total: 
Categories: beet, simple
Ingredients:
	200g cooked beetroot
	2 tbsp olive oil
	Salt and pepper
	4 tbsp basalmic vinegar
	Sheep milk yogurt

Instructions:
	Cut the beetroot into cubes, place in a food processor and blend. Add the olive oil and a litte bit of water until mixture reaches the right consistency. Season to taste, add vinegar. Arrange a spoon of sheep milk yogurt in the bottom of a small glass, then top with the beetroot gespacho.
